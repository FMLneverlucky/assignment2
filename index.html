Ant colonies and behaviour

Ant colonies consists of a queen and worker ants. A colony starts with a queen ant spawning and raising a group of worker ants. These worker ants are in charge of gathering food and feeding the queen so it can focus on reproducing and increasing their numbers. Ants communicate chemically, leaving phermones around their target for other worker ants.
